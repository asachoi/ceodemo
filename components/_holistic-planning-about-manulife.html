  • Manulife’s vision in Asia is to help our customers fulfill their dreams and aspirations.
  • Our mission is simple – we want to deliver an extraordinary customer experience.
  • Our strategy is based on 3 global themes.

1. Build long-lasting customer relationships

Our 118-year heritage in Asia gives us a strong understanding of the 11 markets we operate in. We have one of the longest histories of continuous operation in the region of any international insurer.

This experience allows us to adopt unique approaches to help all our customers and put them at the centre of everything we do.

2. Build our integrated wealth and asset management capabilities

We have integrated wealth and asset management capabilities that few insurance companies in Asia have.

This enables us to provide a tailored and wide-range of financial solutions to meet our customers’ protection, wealth and retirement needs.

3. Leverage our global capabilities

Manulife is a leading Canada-based financial services organization with principal operations in Asia, Canada and the United States.

With market-leading positions across all regions, we can draw on our expertise from our markets to roll out ideas, solutions and capabilities to help our customers around the world.
